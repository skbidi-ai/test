WhatPet.GG Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our platform and services. By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

1. Introduction

WhatPet.GG (we, us, our, the website, or the platform) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website (WhatPet.GG).

We comply with the requirements of COPPA (United States) and GDPR (European Union) for data protection.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

a) Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including: your Roblox ID, username, avatar, or other public profile information; your Discord username (if provided); reports and inquiries you submit to our support team; messages you send in our platform's chat system; and your email address (if provided).

b) Technical Data Collected Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect certain technical information, including: device type, operating system, and browser; IP address; interface language; date and time of your visit; pages you visit on our site; and cookies and other tracking technologies.

3. How We Use This Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes: to authenticate you on our platform; to display your profile; for statistics and to improve user experience; to show advertising, which may be personalized; and to protect against abuse and prevent technical errors.

4. Sharing Data with Third Parties

In certain situations, we may share portions of your data with third parties to improve our platform, including:

Remote Secure Servers and Cloud Services

We use secure servers and cloud services for the safe storage of the website and data.

Analytics Services

We use analytics services such as Google Analytics. Anonymized data is shared to improve the platform, including but not limited to: device type and browser; time spent on page; pages you visited; interaction with the platform (such as clicks and scrolling); geographic location (based on anonymized IP address); and referral source (such as search engines or social networks).

5. Data Storage and Protection

Your data is stored on secure servers. Data transmission occurs through a secure connection (HTTPS). Data may be deleted at any time upon user request.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to: request a copy of their data; delete or modify their data; withdraw consent for data processing by contacting us using the contact information provided below; and opt out of the use of cookies for analytics and advertising (this can be done in your browser settings).

7. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies for the proper functioning of the site, authentication, analytics, and advertising.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, however some features of the platform may not function correctly.

8. Age Restrictions

The platform may be used by children, however we do not collect personal information such as physical address or real name.

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us and we will delete that information.

We strive to comply with the requirements of COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the platform changes or when legal requirements change.

The current version is always available on this page.
